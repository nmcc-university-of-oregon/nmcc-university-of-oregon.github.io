New Media + Culture Certificate

Prof Pick: Tara Fickle


Check out Tara Fickle's winter course on Games as Theory and Culture. If her gamified course description doesn't convince you to add the class, her amazing "game manual" (a syllabus including achievements, a guide to questing, and more) certainly will. Read more on our blog.
 

Shelfie: Jeremiah Favara


Meet NMCC student Jeremiah Favara, a media studies PhD candidate writing an amazing dissertation about US military recruiting advertisements. Jeremiah will give a Research Talk on December 2. Read more about Jeremiah's new media book shelf and how he discovered NMCC on our blog.
 

Farewell to John Russell
 

NMCC bids a fond farewell to John Russell of the Digital Scholarship Center. John starts a new job next term as Associate Director of the Center for Humanities and Information at Penn State. Thank you for everything, John-- you'll be missed!
