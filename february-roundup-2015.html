New Media + Culture Certificate

 

NMCC Course Spotlight: Habitual New Media

Colin Koopman (Philosophy) and Wendy Hui Kyong Chun (2014-15 Wayne Morse Chair of Law and Politics) co-taught a NMCC-sponsored seminar entitled "Habitual New Media" during Winter 2015.

Professor Koopman and student Bonnie Sheehey (PhD, Philosophy) shared their thoughts with us about the course as well as the related Living Data: Inhabiting New Media symposium. Read more.

 

Prof Picks: Daniel Steinhart
Assistant Professor, SOJC

Prof. Steinhart's current research investigates post-World War II Hollywood productions filmed around the world. In this feature, Professor Steinhart discusses his research into the art and industry of cinema and shares links to selected new media resources that he finds useful in his daily research. Read more.

 

Shelfie: Alec Tefertiller
PhD student, Media Studies


Alec discusses what first attracted to him to NMCC even before he arrived at UO this fall. His fascinating research investigates streaming media, in particular looking at trends such as "connected viewing" and "binge watching" habits on sites such as Netflix and Amazon, and how these trends are changing our notion of media attendance. Read more.
