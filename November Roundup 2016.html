November Prof Pick: Andrew Bonamici

Dean Bonamici holds many 'new media' hats here at the university, anchoring Special Collections, University Archives, and the Digital Scholarship Center at the Knight Library. Read more about him and get his picks for go-to twitter feeds on our blog.



Shelfie: Andrew O. McLaughlin
What's on your new media bookshelf? NMCC student Andrew McLaughlin's "shelfie" showcases books and resources focused on filmmaking and film history, specifically those on cult film and esoteric histories that are not easily accessible. Learn more about Andrew's research on our blog.
