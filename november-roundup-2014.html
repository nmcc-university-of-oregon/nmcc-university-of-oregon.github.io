NMCC Shelfies 
Shelfies are selfies (informal self-portraits) that show NMCC 
students and faculty in their native habitats: surrounded by 
bookshelves or enmeshed in other media workspaces.

NMCC's first Shelfie feature is Laurette Garner, 
MA candidate, Arts Management. Hint: her workspace involves 
dressing up as a gorilla! 

To learn more about what being a new media scholar means to Laurette, read more on our blog. 

Have a shelfie to share? Contact us.
 

 
 


Get to Know the Digital Scholarship Center

The UO Digital Scholarship Center, located in 142 Knight Library, works with students and faculty to transform research, scholarly communication, and instruction using new media and technologies.

The staff works closely with the NMCC and frequently collaborates with grad students. What are the top five things you should know about the DSC? Find out on our blog.
