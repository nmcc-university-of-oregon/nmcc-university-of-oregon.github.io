Erkki Huhtamo Lecture on Screenology
Mark your calendars for the School of Architecture and Allied Arts Interdisciplinary Lecture. One of the founding figures of Media Archaeology, Erkki Huhtamo, will give a talk on "Screenology, or Media Archaeology of the Screen" on February 27 at 6:00 pm, LA 177.


Feminist Media Studies Symposium
Internationally renowned feminist media scholar and University of Oregon professor Kathleen Rowe Karlyn will be honored by the Cinema Studies Program, Saturday, February 11th, at the Feminist Media Studies Symposium.
