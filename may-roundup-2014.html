New Media + Culture Certificate


Congratulations 2014 NMCC Graduates!

We are delighted to announce the first group of grad students to earn the New Media and Culture Certificate: Jacob Levernier (Psychology), Jenny Dean (Communication & Society), Brant Burkey (Media Studies), Emily McGinn (Comparative Literature), Ryan Eanes (Media Studies), Jeremy Swartz (Communication & Society), and Iris Bull (Media Studies).  Visit our announcement online to learn more about these students and their post-graduation plans.



NMC Summer Conference

The NMC Summer Conference will be held nearby in Portland, OR this year from June 16-19. The conference attracts highly skilled professionals interested in the integration of emerging technologies into teaching, learning, and creative inquiry. Learn more about this year's program on our blog.
 


Fall 2014 Courses

Fall 2014 NMCC courses are now online. Please visit our academic website for an up-to-date list. These pre-approved classes are just the beginning, however--NMCC students may also fulfill Certificate requirements through our active petition process.
