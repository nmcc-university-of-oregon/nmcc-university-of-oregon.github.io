New Media + Culture Certificate


Prof Picks: Michael Allan

Michael Allan's new fall seminar in "Transmedial Aesthetics" (COLT 616) explores how we read in relation to different forms of media. Not surprisingly, his course is a huge hit with NMCC students! To learn more about the course and Prof. Allan's other "picks," visit our blog.

 


Student Shelfie: Tara Burke

 
Meet Tara Burke-- an arts management student who is pursuing the NMCC to supplement her expertise in community arts. Visit our blog to learn more about Tara's favorite new media resources and what her upcoming year looks like (hint: it involves a lot of music festivals!).
 



NMCC Fall Open House

Friday, October 30th
9:30-10:30 am
Digital Scholarship Center, 142 Knight Library

Please join us for an informal meet-and-greet to discuss all things "new media" over breakfast treats and coffee. The event provides an opportunity to mingle with other grads, faculty, and staff involved with new media and culture initiatives across campus. All are welcome and prospective students are especially encouraged to attend.
